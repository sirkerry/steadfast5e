# Steadfast5e - Proficiency Bonus Cap +4

Part of the [Steadfast5e](https://forge.fantasygrounds.com/) suite for grittier, OSR-style 5E play.

## What it does

Replaces the standard 5E proficiency bonus progression (which scales to +6 at level 17+) with a slower, capped progression that tops out at +4:

| Character Level | Proficiency Bonus |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| 1–6             | +2                |
| 7–12            | +3                |
| 13–20           | +4                |

The bonus is recalculated automatically whenever character level changes. No manual adjustments needed.

## Why

The standard progression to +6 inflates attack rolls, save DCs, and skill checks at high levels in ways that can undermine the tension of the game. Capping at +4 keeps those numbers tighter across the full level range, making encounters more consistently dangerous and skills more fallible.

## Compatibility

- D&D 5E 2014 (Legacy) and 2024
- Compatible with other Steadfast5e extensions
- No options panel — it is always active when loaded

## Installation

Drop the `steadfast5e_prof_cap` folder into your Fantasy Grounds Unity `extensions/` directory and enable it when loading a 5E campaign.
